About me

Hi, I'm Stephen Kolbo. A playboy billionaire by day, my double life affords me the comfort of a life without financial worry.

I have a loyal butler-turned-guardian named Alfred and the perfect base of operations in an ancient network of caves beneath my family’s sprawling estate.

By night, however, I shed all pretense, don my iconic scalloped cape and pointed cowl and I take to the shadowy streets, skies and rooftops of Gotham City. I am vengeance, I am the night, I am a future web designer!
